About ATBI

Agri-Aqua Technology Business Incubator (ATBI)

The CapSU Agri-Aqua Technology Business Incubator (ATBI) is an initiative that provides support to agripreneurs, innovators, and researchers in developing sustainable agri-aqua-based enterprises. The ATBI serves as a nurturing ground where ideas are transformed into viable business ventures, promoting innovation and economic growth in the agricultural and aquaculture sectors.

The program focuses on assisting startups and small enterprises by offering mentorship, training, and access to facilities and technologies developed by Capiz State University. Through collaboration with experts, entrepreneurs, and investors, ATBI aims to strengthen the local agribusiness ecosystem and empower communities.

Our Mission

To cultivate innovation and entrepreneurship in agriculture and aquaculture by providing resources, mentorship, and opportunities for startups to thrive and create sustainable impact.

Our Vision

To be a leading incubator hub that fosters agri-aqua technology innovation and transforms research-based ideas into globally competitive enterprises.
