About ATBI

The Agri-Aqua Technology Business Incubator (ATBI) is dedicated to supporting innovative entrepreneurs in agribusiness and aquaculture. We provide mentorship, technical support, and market access to help startups grow and thrive.

Our mission is to promote sustainable food systems and empower local farmers, innovators, and communities through innovation and collaboration.

ATBI partners with universities, government, and private sectors to transform research and ideas into viable, market-ready solutions.
