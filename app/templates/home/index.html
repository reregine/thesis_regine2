About ATBI

The Agri-Aqua Technology Business Incubator (ATBI) is a platform dedicated to nurturing agribusiness and aquaculture startups. We provide mentorship, technical assistance, and access to sustainable market opportunities.

Our goal is to foster innovation, sustainability, and food security through collaboration with universities, experts, and local entrepreneurs.

ATBI continues to empower innovators to create positive social and economic impacts within the agriculture and aquaculture sectors.
