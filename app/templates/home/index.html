About ATBI

The Agri-Aqua Technology Business Incubator (ATBI) supports local innovators and startups in agriculture and aquaculture through technical mentorship, business development, and access to market opportunities.

Our mission is to empower entrepreneurs to promote sustainable growth, innovation, and food security. ATBI serves as a bridge between researchers, universities, and industries to accelerate Agri-Aqua technologies.

With continuous support from DOST-PCAARRD, ATBI helps transform ideas into viable enterprises that create real community impact.
