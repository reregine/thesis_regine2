Discover our mission to cultivate innovation and entrepreneurship in agriculture and aquaculture

Agri-Aqua Technology Business Incubator (ATBI)

The CapSU Agri-Aqua Technology Business Incubator (ATBI) is an innovative initiative that provides comprehensive support to agripreneurs, innovators, and researchers in developing sustainable agri-aqua-based enterprises. The ATBI serves as a nurturing ground where ideas are transformed into viable business ventures, promoting innovation and economic growth in the agricultural and aquaculture sectors.

The program focuses on assisting startups and small enterprises by offering mentorship, training, and access to state-of-the-art facilities and technologies developed by Capiz State University. Through strategic collaboration with industry experts, entrepreneurs, and investors, ATBI aims to strengthen the local agribusiness ecosystem and empower communities toward sustainable development.

Our Mission

To cultivate innovation and entrepreneurship in agriculture and aquaculture by providing resources, mentorship, and opportunities for startups to thrive and create sustainable impact in local and global markets.

Our Vision

To be a leading incubator hub that fosters agri-aqua technology innovation and transforms research-based ideas into globally competitive enterprises that drive economic growth and environmental sustainability.

Our Core Values

Innovation

Pioneering new approaches to agri-aqua challenges through technology and creativity.

Collaboration

Building strong partnerships between academia, industry, and communities.

Sustainability

Promoting environmentally responsible and economically viable solutions.

Empowerment

Equipping entrepreneurs with knowledge, skills, and resources for success.
